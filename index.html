Baking recipe for Mailänder

Ingredients

Butter: 250 g

Sugar : 225 g

Salt : 1 pinch

Eggs : 3

Lemon : 1

Flour : 500 g

Egg yolk: 1

Instructions

1.Step

The first thing you need to do is prepare all the ingredients so that you don't forget anyt hing when baking.
It is best to make your workspace as empty as possible to have enough space for baking.

2.Step

Now you have to mix the soft butter with a pinch of salt and sugar together.
The best way to do this is with a mixer.

3.Step

Now you have to add the three eggs to the sugar and butter.
You should also do this step with a mixer.

4.Step

Next, grate in a little lemon zest.
This will give the Mailänder a little more flavour.

5.Step

Now you have to add to the mix the flour.
you should do it with your hands. If the dough is too sticky, add more flour.

6.Step

After you have finished kneading the dough, put the foil over it.
The dough must now be in the cold for about 2 hours.

7.Step

Take out the dough and roll it out. Make it about the size of your little finger.
Take your favourite moulds and make the biscuits.

8.Step

Spread your biscuits on the baking tray. Brush them with egg yolk and put them in the oven.
The biscuits should be in the oven for about 10 minutes at 200 degrees.

9.Step

Enjoy your Mailänderli:)
